About Fresy

Village Freshness Delivered 🌿

Who We Are

Fresy is a grocery delivery platform focused on providing fresh vegetables, fruits, and daily essentials directly to your home. We connect village freshness with modern convenience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver quality products at affordable prices while supporting local farmers and small businesses.

Why Choose Fresy?

  • ✔ Fresh & Quality Products
  • ✔ Affordable Pricing
  • ✔ Fast Home Delivery
  • ✔ Trusted Local Service

Meaning of Fresy

The name Fresy comes from the idea of Fresh + Easy.

It represents our promise to deliver fresh groceries with an easy and smooth shopping experience.

Meet Our Founder

Priyam Mete

Priyam Mete is the founder of Fresy. He started Fresy with a vision to bring village freshness directly to every household using technology.

As a young entrepreneur and his goal is to build a trusted grocery delivery platform for villages and cities.
